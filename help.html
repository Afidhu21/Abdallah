ABOUT OUR HELPS

Customers can comment or give feedback to Tesla through a few official and unofficial channels. Here's how you can do it:
1. Through the Tesla Mobile App (for Owners)
Open the Tesla app
Tap Service
Select a relevant issue, then scroll down to add comments or describe feedback in the notes section when scheduling service.
You can also use the Roadside Assistance or Help sections to report problems or experiences.
2. Tesla Website Feedback
Tesla doesn’t have a public feedback form, but for general communication you can:
Visit https://www.tesla.com/contact
Choose the relevant category (sales, delivery, energy, etc.)
Fill in your message or feedback
3. Social Media
Tesla (and Elon Musk) often pay attention to customer comments on platforms like:
X (Twitter): Tweet @Tesla or @ElonMusk
Reddit: r/TeslaMotors is an active Tesla community
YouTube/Instagram: Comment on official Tesla videos or posts
Many customers share reviews, complaints, and ideas this way. Sometimes they even get direct responses.
4. Post-Delivery Surveys
After purchasing a Tesla, customers often receive email surveys about their delivery, service, and vehicle experience.
This is a formal way Tesla gathers user feedback
5. Tesla Forums (Now Archived)
Tesla used to have official forums, but they've been mostly closed. Now, third-party forums like:
Tesla Motors Club (https://teslamotorsclub.com)
Reddit’s r/TeslaMotors …are widely used for discussions and indirect feedback.
