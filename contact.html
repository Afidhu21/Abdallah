If you're looking to get help or support from Tesla, whether as a customer, investor,
or job applicant, here’s a clear breakdown of how to do it:

1. For Customers (Vehicle or Energy Products)
Ways to Get Help:
Tesla App (Best Option for Owners):
If you own a Tesla vehicle or Powerwall, use the Tesla mobile app.
You can schedule service, contact support, or report issues.

Tesla Support Website:
Visit https://www.tesla.com/support
Covers FAQs about charging, software updates, service centers, delivery, financing, etc.
Call Tesla Support (Region-Specific Numbers):
In the U.S., call 1-888-518-3752
Other countries have local support lines listed on Tesla's website.
Service Center Visits:
Schedule service through the Tesla app—walk-ins are not accepted.


2. For Investors
Visit https://ir.tesla.com for:
Investor relations
Earnings reports
Press releases
Stock information

Tesla doesn’t have individual investor support by phone, but you can email:
ir@tesla.com


3. For Job Seekers
Visit Tesla's Careers Page: https://www.tesla.com/careers
